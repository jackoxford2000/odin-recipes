Barbecue Turkey

Description

Have you accidentally run over a dog? Look no further! With this recipe, you can transform the dog into delicious barbecue turkey.

NOTE: Feedback on this recipe may vary dependent on the dog used...

Ingredients

  • Dog

Instructions

  1. Find a clearing, for example in a forest.
  2. Place the dog upon your barbecue, and light it - this is best with fire lighters. Make sure your friend buys them...
  3. Cook until well-burnt.
  4. Place in a bin bag, then consume ASAP.
