Moroccan Pasta

Description

Throwing an impromptu dinner party and have hardly anything in the cupboard? Look no further than this delicious, traditional Moroccan pasta!

Inspired by the culinary genius of Mark Corrigan, author of "Business Secrets of the Pharaohs", an international bestseller.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Toast
  • Lettuce
  • Eggs
  • Beans

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta, eggs, toast, lettuce, anything else you can find for an authentic experience.
  2. Stir it all together in a large dish.
  3. Bake in the oven until your guests have arrived.
  4. Serve whilst still hot!
